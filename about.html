About Me

I am a first-year MS student in the Applied Mathematics and Statistics program with a focus on Quantitative Finance at Stony Brook University. I am an aspiring Quantitative Analyst and enjoy applying machine learning and statistical methods to the field of finance. My academic and personal projects explore areas like stochastic modeling, reinforcement learning, and time series analysis.

Before joining Stony Brook University, I graduated from Hamilton College with a B.A. in Mathematics and Computer Science. At Hamilton, my studies consisted of statistical theory, data science, and programming, while my extracurricular activities included being the captain of the football team. I am driven by curiosity and love solving real-world problems with elegant mathematical solutions.
