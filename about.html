About Me

I am a first-year MS student in the Applied Mathematics and Statistics program at Stony Brook University, concentrating in Quantitative Finance. I enjoy applying machine learning and statistical methods to the field of finance. My academic and personal projects explore areas like stochastic modeling, machine learning, and time series analysis.

In May of 2025, I graduated from Hamilton College with a B.A. in Mathematics and Computer Science. My studies consisted of statistical theory, data science, and programming. In addition to academics, I served as captain of the Hamilton College Football Team and president of the Psi Upsilon Psi Chapter. I am driven by curiosity and love solving real-world problems with elegant mathematical solutions.
